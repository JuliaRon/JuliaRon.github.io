Being Me Digital Workshops

In February 2025, I co-developed and co-facilitated a series of workshops and after-school clubs called Being Me Digital. This happened in collaboration with other freelancers and with Can't Sit Still - A Bristol based charity, that uses creativity and culture to disrupt social inequalities; inspiring individuals, connecting communities and spreading joy.

Being Me Digital is an offer for girls and non-binary young people with autism to spend some time thinking about, crafting and digitizing a space in which they would like to be. We're asking what individuals need to feel safe, to feel bubbly or to experience sensory joy.
In a four day workshop series and multiple after-schools clubs, participants had access to crafting materials, inspirations, as well as a space where it's okay to just be you! Throughout, we introduced a range of different digital tools, such as 3D scanning, motion capture, AI avatar creation, and AR Image Target tracking.
I truely loved the vibe of the sessions, meeting a bunch of wonderful people and creating my personal space alongside the participants. I learned a lot about autism, about different ways of being creative and about introducing digital tools and ideas to young people.
