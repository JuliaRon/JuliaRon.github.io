I'm a freelance Creative Technologist and Unity Developer with a passion for building immersive and interactive XR experiences that spark wonder and joy. I love using technology to blur the lines between the physical and virtual, crafting engaging narratives and exploring the full potential of immersive mediums.

My background in Computer Science and Maths, along with a Master's in Virtual and Extended Realities, allows me to combine technical expertise with artistic vision to bring innovative projects to life. From developing hand-tracking integrations at Ultraleap to contributing to the award-winning VR experience "Goliath" and exploring passive haptics in "Cognition", I'm always excited to take on new challenges and collaborate on impactful projects.
